Empathic Link (Su) An oaken defender has an empathic link with the dryads of its grove, through which it can sense their needs and feelings.  
This link extends up to 900 feet.
Find Oaken Defender (Su) As the discern location spell; always active; caster level 18th. An oaken defender can use this ability only to find another oaken defender on the same plane as itself. All oaken defenders are considered to have seen one another for the purpose of this ability.
